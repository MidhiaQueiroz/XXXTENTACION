About XXXTENTACION

XXXTENTACION, also known as Jahseh Dwayne Ricardo Onfory, was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. Jahseh was born in Plantation, Florida, United States, on January 23, 1998. Jahseh was fatally shot on June 18, 2018, in Deerfield Beach, Florida, while shopping at a motorcycle dealership. He was in his vehicle leaving the dealership, when a black van stopped in front of his car and two men exited with weapons and proceeded to rob and murder Jahseh.

Growing up

Jahseh grew up in a small city in Florida called Broward County. His interest in music began early in his teen years, at around 13 years old. Because of the divorce from his parents when he was about 2 years of age, Jahseh was always a troubled kid. He got into a lot of trouble while attending school, due to fights and trouble with teachers. He also got into trouble with the law a few times, due to robbery, drugs, etc. This ended up getting him into a Juvenile Detention Center, where he met his friend and rapper Ski Mask the Slump, God. Jahseh dropped out of high school to pursue his music career, alongside Ski Mask.

Diversity of Music

Jahseh was an extremely diverse artist, he was able to produce hip-hop, pop, rock, R&B etc. He was heavily referred to as a rapper, though he demonstrated through his projects that he was capable of many genres. An example of a project that showed these capabilities, is his 2018 album "?" which included 18 tracks. From start to finish the album showed tracks of all types of genres. On the other hand, the album "17", released in August 2017, was a slowed-down album showing the more emotional side of Jahseh.

Projects/Albums

As mentioned previously, Jahseh was an extremely diverse artist. Going all the way back to 2013, Jahseh released a project called "Mona Lisa" on SoundCloud, which is where all his music was posted. Jahseh's first major project was released in May 2017, called "Revenge". This was essentially a mixtape with 7 tracks, most of which were already released onto his SoundCloud. Not long after an album was released including Jahseh and Members Only (friends of XXXTENTACION) called Members Only Vol. 3 (MOV3). Then, "17" was released in the summer of 2017, which gained a lot of attraction because of how different the project was from his previous work. "?" was released in March 2018, also attaining much attraction because of the album's diversity. Also, Jahseh was a lot more known around this time which played into the amount of attraction and listened the album "?" received. This unfortunately was the last piece of work released before Jahseh passed away in June 2018. A couple of other projects were released after his death, such as "Skins" (2018), and "Bad Vibes Forever" (2019).
